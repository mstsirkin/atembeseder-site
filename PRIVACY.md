# Privacy Policy

## Overview

Atem Beseder is designed to help users share their status with trusted people during alerts and emergencies.

## Data We Use

Depending on the features you enable, the app may process:

- selected watched areas
- points of interest you save
- your current location, if you enable location features
- alert data received from remote services
- status data you choose to share

## Google Drive Integration

If you connect Google Drive, the app may create and update a file in your own Google Drive account to store your shared status data. The app only uses the access you explicitly grant.

## Notifications

The app may use push notifications and background checks to deliver alert updates.

## Data Sharing

The app only sends data to external services that are required for alert delivery or that you explicitly configure or authorize.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, use the contact details published with the project.
